FAQ

Common questions

Which cloud providers do you support?

We work with AWS, GCP, Hetzner, DigitalOcean, and bare-metal providers. If you're on something else, ask us — we've probably worked with it.

What's your typical response time for incidents?

P1 incidents (site down, data loss risk): 15 minutes. P2 (degraded performance): 1 hour. We're on-call, not just office hours.

Do you require long-term contracts?

Managed plans are billed monthly with a 30-day notice period. We'd rather earn your business every month than lock you in.

Can you work alongside our internal team?

Yes — many clients have developers who handle application code while we handle the infrastructure layer. We define a clear boundary of responsibility upfront.
